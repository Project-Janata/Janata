IA options — the two entry doors

Three genuinely different ways to structure the entry. Each shows the two doors as phone wireframes (desktop = same regions, centered max-width card / 2-col content). Grayscale on purpose — judge structure, not style. Numbered dots = inventory IDs.

Real-app evidence: Door 1 vouch pattern — Clubhouse ("X invited you" + inviter face, single Accept), Bluesky, Partiful. Door 2 browse-first — Luma, Meetup, Eventbrite show real events immediately with a location pill refiner and just-in-time auth (the account ask fires at RSVP, never at the door).

Locked from your answers: Door 1 = one strong screen (vouch + one-line what-it-is + Accept), extra slides optional. Door 2 = real events/centers near them first, location-shaped, RSVP & invite secondary.

A — Door-routedintent-first: the link picks the doorRecommended

The link itself routes. An invite link opens a purpose-built vouch screen; a cold open lands on a purpose-built browse screen. Two clean entries, each tuned for its user. Account/RSVP is summoned just-in-time from inside either door — never a shared funnel up front.
